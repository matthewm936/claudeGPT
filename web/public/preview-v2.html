That question — "are my values mine or are they reactions" — is one of the most important questions a person can sit with. And the honest answer is: both. Every value you hold was forged in response to something. The question isn't whether they're reactive. It's whether you've examined the reaction closely enough to choose it consciously. There's a difference between "I value independence because I was never given it" and "I value independence because I examined why I crave it, traced it back to its origin, and decided it's still worth holding." The content is the same. The relationship to it is entirely different. One is a reflex. The other is a position.
